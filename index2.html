Our Story


Jun 23, 2013 - Meeting
Cong and Margi met at an All-You-Can-Eat hotpot in Boston through Allie. After a quick introduction, Cong proceeded to get his money’s worth of meat. Margi did interact with him by teaching him how to make a crane chopstick holder though. We took a group photo that day. Notice Cong’s awkward hand on Margi’s shoulder.
Jun 30, 2013 - Hitting it off
Before Allie moved to CA, she wanted to go clubbing in NYC. She brought Margi and another friend. That night, Cong and Margi drank and hit it off. Cong was very drunk. Margi was fine. Cong thought Margi was a tank. (Margi actually poured herself ⅕ shots) Cong and Margi started talking soon after :)
Oct 4, 2013 - “Be my GF?”
At the end of summer, Cong went back to Berkeley to finish his last year in college, while Margi started working full time in Boston. Still, they couldn't stop talking! When Margi visited Cong for the first time, he took her to the hills near Lawrence Hall of the Science, and asked her out with a letter and a jar of origami cranes. She said yes!
Aug 2014 - South Bay life
After a year of skyping and flying to see each other every few months, Margi moved out to CA and Cong started his new job in Mountain View! Together, over the next 4 years, they settled into the typical-but-blissful, surburbian South Bay life. They played board games (favorite being Dominion), hung out and travelled with friends, and occasionally celebrated at fancy restaurants.
May 2017 - Ring shopping
Cong decided to grow up and embark on the next step of adult life. First, Cong and his friend Boss embarked on this journey of understanding “engagement rings”. Cong learned about the 4 C’s while Boss helped book appointments at very fancy jewelry shops. After 6 months, Cong finally bought the ring! Now that Cong had the ring, he needed to mentally prepare to propose. Sadly, Boss couldn’t help with this.
Jul 2017 - Proposal planning
Cong and Boss started planning the epic proposal. Cong decided to propose where he originally asked her out, at the hills near Lawrence Hall of the Science! The plan became more elaborate as friends were invited and Cong secretly flew out Margi’s two best friends to attend in person. The weekend before, they scouted out the location, established optimal positioning for photos, and finalized details (i.e. which knee to propose on). Cong prepared and practiced a speech all week.
Dec 9, 2017 - The proposal
Margi made the proposal more stressful by feeling sick all week and even wanting to skip the mandatory “lunch” we planned. Luckily, the plan was still a go. Everything went relatively to plan: Cong and Margi had lunch in Berkeley with friends, they end up to the Lawrence Hall of Science, there was a table set up with a letter and flower, Cong delivered the crappiest version of his speech, Michael took great pictures, and Margi said yes :) Other friends showed up in surprise and Margi cried the most when she saw Susie and Yukun (Yes, more than Cong proposing). Everyone celebrated with dinner together at night.
Dec 2017 - Engaged!
Fiancee was a very strange term that Cong and Margi soon grew accustomed to saying. Wedding planning began and it was more stressful than Cong imagined. And a year after Cong and Margi got engaged, they bought a house and finally moved in together! They were excited for home ownership. Cong is happy he finally has a garage for his car (soon to be cars). Margi is happy she can upgrade everything HGTV style.

FAQ

❀ What time should I arrive at the ceremony?
Doors open at 3:30pm so please arrive then to ensure that there's enough time to park and walk to the ceremony site. The ceremony will begin promptly at 4:00pm.
❀ When should I RSVP by?
Please RSVP by 4/15/19.
❀ What's the dress code?
Semi-Formal/Cocktail Attire. Ladies - please make note that part of the day's festivities is outdoors and may involve walking on lawns and hilly areas, so choose your footwear accordingly! It may also be chilly so please consider bringing warm layers.
❀ Is there parking for the ceremony/reception?
Parking is available on site, but limited. We appreciate the group effort in carpooling if that is an option for you!
❀ Is Wi-Fi available?
Feel free to connect to wi-fi 'nestldown' as some carriers may have poor connection at the venue.
❀ Should I take an Uber/Lyft to the venue?
Oddly enough, these services only go to the venue during the day and not at night. Please consider carpooling.
❀ Is there a registry?
No, we do not have a registry. We both have more “things” than we know what to do with, and your presence at our wedding is all the gift we could ever want. We'll have a guestbook and a box for cards if you would like to leave us a note!

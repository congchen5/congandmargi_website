FAQ

Q: What time should I arrive at the ceremony?
A: Doors open at 3:30pm so please arrive then to ensure that there's enough time to park and walk to the ceremony site. The ceremony will begin promptly at 4:00pm.
Q: When should I RSVP by?
A: Please RSVP by 4/15/19.
Q: What's the dress code?
A: Semi-Formal/Cocktail Attire. Ladies - please make note that part of the day's festivities is outdoors and may involve walking on lawns and hilly areas, so choose your footwear accordingly! It may also be chilly so please consider bringing warm layers.
Q: Is there parking for the ceremony/reception?
A: Parking is available on site, but limited. We appreciate the group effort in carpooling if that is an option for you!
Q: Is Wi-Fi available?
A: Feel free to connect to wi-fi 'nestldown' as some carriers may have poor connection at the venue.
Q: Should I take an Uber/Lyft to the venue?
A: Oddly enough, these services only go to the venue during the day and not at night. Please consider carpooling.
Q: Is there a registry?
A: No, we do not have a registry. We both have more “things” than we know what to do with, and your presence at our wedding is all the gift we could ever want. We'll have a guestbook and a box for cards if you would like to leave us a note!
